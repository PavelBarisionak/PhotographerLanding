About me. Sam Smith

My Photographic journey started on 2007. While on a trip to Glencoe, I captured an image of stag at the side of the road, it was not until I got home and transferred the image onto my pc that I thought I had captured some thing a bit special and I started taking an interest in photography and wanted to know how to take better photographs. My photography has always been part time, arranging shoots around my work. I am still photographing weddings, landscapes and other events, I am also now improving my image editing techniques using programs like photoshop and lightroom to take my photography to the next level. I now like creating images by blending two or more images together.
